Privacy & Safety Notice

This page explains what data Neural Maze Runner uses so students, parents, and teachers can make informed decisions.

What This App Does Not Collect

  • No student account signup.
  • No name, email, phone number, or photo collection.
  • No camera, microphone, or geolocation access.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs in app code.

What Is Stored In Your Browser

The app stores learning progress and settings on this device using browser storage (localStorage).

  • Mission progress and badges.
  • Coach/quiz state and stars.
  • Some UI preferences for lessons.

External Links

An external company link may be shown on the landing screen. The app asks for confirmation before leaving this site.

Students should ask a parent or teacher before opening any external link.

Reset / Delete Local Data

  • Use the in-app reset controls when available.
  • Or clear site data in the browser settings for this website.

For Schools

If your school has policy requirements (COPPA/FERPA/state rules), have an administrator or teacher review this notice before classroom rollout.
